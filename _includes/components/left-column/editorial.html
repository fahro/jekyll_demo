Our editors help journalists across the OCCRP network turn extremely complicated investigations into stories that readers around the world can understand, share, and act upon. We also coordinate large cross-border investigations, bringing together different journalists and member centers and ensuring that project members are equipped with the right technology, data, and security tools. All journalists in the OCCRP network are invited to pitch stories to our core editorial team in Sarajevo, which provides feedback and editorial support both pre- and post-publication. Our daily news team also produces English-language news coverage of organized crime, corruption, and issues facing journalists worldwide, while our engagement section amplifies the work of our member centers.
