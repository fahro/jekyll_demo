ID is an OCCRP service that helps investigative journalists within the network conduct research quickly and effectively. A team of researchers is on hand to track down people, companies, and assets — like ships and planes — anywhere in the world. They are equipped with access to a number of commercial search engines and databases, as well as the experience to make effective use of dozens of publicly available, but little-known, open sources. With the support of a bespoke ticketing system to shorten the lead time from story inception to publication, the research team helps OCCRP members break new ground in investigative journalism.

Got a research request? Submit a ticket id.occrp.org
